Industrial/Energy

From the beginning, our work in the Industrial/Energy sectors has been critical to the growth of our entire business. We have been instrumental in building and maintaining many successful oilfield, pipeline, and industrial facilities in southwest Wyoming. And with our ongoing commitment to safety, quality, and the environment, we have expanded our work in industrial/energy throughout the greater Rocky Mountain Region.

Our services include:

  • Lateral and flow-line placement
  • Meter station installation
  • Meter building and foundation construction
  • Launcher and receiver barrel installation and testing
  • Boring
  • Hydrostatic testing
  • Painting and coating
  • Anomaly repair
  • Hydro excavation
  • Regulator station construction
  • Pipeline installation
  • Pump and compressor installation
  • ROW remediation and erosion control
  • Electrical and instrumentation services
  • Cathodic protection

Industries we serve include:

  • Oil and Gas
  • Power Generation
  • Power Distribution
  • Raw Cement Production
  • Mining
  • Clean Energy
