Awards & Recognition

Our greatest reward is repeat, long-term business from customers that hire Flare year after year to deliver safe, complex, on-time projects.

This commitment to safe and successful projects is further confirmed by the awards and recognition we have earned through the years.

UDOT 2012 Small Contractor of the Year Award

Submitted by: Jeff Roberts, Field Engineer for Project Engineering Consultants of West Jordan This award was based on Flare’s safety, ethics, partnering, communication, commitment to quality, compliance, and project closeout on the Echo Trestle Historic Rail Trail Extension in northern Utah.
