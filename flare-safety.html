Safety

Safety is the cornerstone of all Flare operations and is a priority in every aspect of our business. The company places the highest value on the safety of its teams and continually strives for an accident-free workplace. At Flare, safety is a way of life on and off the job and employees are empowered to stop any unsafe act. Our safety department works with each project team and client to identify/mitigate hazards, provide safety equipment, perform audits and provide training to prevent accidents.
