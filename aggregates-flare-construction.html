Aggregates

Flare Construction has two aggregate pits in operation — our Bald Rock Pit located in Henefer, Utah, and the Faddis Pit in Evanston, Wyoming. Both pits produce high-quality, crushed rock products that have been independently tested to meet UDOT and WYDOT standards.
